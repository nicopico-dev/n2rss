Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Newsletters to RSS (n2rss). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application. By using n2rss, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Data Collection

We collect data for analytics purposes to help us understand how our service is being used and to improve it. The data collected includes:

  • Home Page Access: When you access the Home page, we collect the user-agent of your browser and the timestamp of your visit.
  • JSON Data Retrieval: When you retrieve JSON data about the available RSS feeds, we collect the user-agent of your browser and the timestamp of the access.
  • RSS Feed Access: When you access an RSS feed, we collect the identifier of the RSS feed, the user-agent of your browser, and the timestamp of the access.
  • Newsletter Support Request: When you request support for a newsletter, we collect the URL associated with the newsletter, the user-agent of your browser, and the timestamp of the request.

2. Use of Data

The data collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our service.
  • To monitor the performance and reliability of the application.
  • To respond to user requests and provide support for additional newsletters.

3. Data Storage

Data collected through the n2rss application is stored securely in our database. We take appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Simple Analytics to collect anonymous usage data for analytics purposes. Simple Analytics does not track personal data and is fully compliant with GDPR and other privacy regulations. For more information, you can visit the Simple Analytics Privacy Policy.

5. Open Source

n2rss is an open-source project, and the source code is available on GitHub at https://github.com/nicopico-dev/n2rss. By contributing to the project or using the code, you should be aware that any contributions may be publicly visible and accessible.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at n2rss-contact@nicopico.fr.
