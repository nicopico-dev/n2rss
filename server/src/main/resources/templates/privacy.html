Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Newsletters to RSS (n2rss). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application. By using n2rss, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Data Collection

We collect data for analytics purposes to help us understand how our service is being used and to improve it. The application collects the following data:

  • Page Access: When you access the home page, we collect your browser's user-agent string and your IP address.
  • JSON API Access: When you retrieve JSON data about available RSS feeds, we collect your browser's user-agent string and your IP address.
  • RSS Feed Access: When you access an RSS feed, we collect the feed identifier (code), your browser's user-agent string, and your IP address.
  • Newsletter Support Request: When you request support for a new newsletter, we collect the newsletter URL you provide, your browser's user-agent string, and your IP address.
  • Error Tracking: When errors occur during email processing, we may collect the email subject line and the name of the newsletter handler that encountered the error.

2. Unique Identifiers

For server-side analytics, we generate a hashed identifier (fingerprint) based on a combination of your user-agent string and IP address. This allows us to track usage patterns without storing personally identifiable information. The fingerprint is a one-way hash that cannot be reversed to obtain your original IP address or user-agent.

3. Use of Data

The data collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To analyze usage patterns and understand how users interact with our service.
  • To monitor the performance, reliability, and availability of the application.
  • To identify popular RSS feeds and usage trends.
  • To respond to user requests for additional newsletter support.
  • To detect and troubleshoot errors in email processing.

4. Analytics Services

We use PostHog for product analytics. PostHog provides us with insights into how our service is being used while respecting user privacy. PostHog's data collection is configured to be privacy-conscious:

  • IP addresses are hashed before storage
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is intentionally collected
  • All data is processed in compliance with GDPR and other applicable privacy regulations

For more information about PostHog's privacy practices, please refer to their Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Data collected through the n2rss application is stored securely. Server-side analytics data is stored in our database with appropriate security measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification. Client-side analytics data is sent to and processed by PostHog's servers located in the European Union.

We implement technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of your data, including: encryption in transit, access controls, and regular security reviews.

6. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for a limited period to allow us to analyze trends and usage patterns. We regularly review and purge old data that is no longer needed for our analytical purposes.

7. Open Source

n2rss is an open-source project, and the source code is available on GitHub at https://github.com/nicopico-dev/n2rss. By contributing to the project or using the code, you should be aware that any contributions may be publicly visible and accessible.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: In certain circumstances, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to request restriction of processing your personal data.
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to processing of your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on this page. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us at n2rss-contact@nicopico.fr.
