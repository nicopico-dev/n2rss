Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/08/2024

Introduction

Welcome to "Newsletters to RSS" (N2RSS), an open-source project designed to provide RSS feeds for a selection of newsletters. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to ensure your data is protected.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our application, we collect the following types of information:

  • Feed Access Information:
    • Timestamp: The time when the feed is accessed.
    • Unique ID of the Feed: An identifier associated with the specific feed being accessed.
    • User-Agent: Information about the software making the HTTP request, which is hashed to identify unique users.
  • Newsletter Request Information:
    • Timestamp: The time when the newsletter is requested.
    • URL Provided by the User: The URL of the newsletter requested.
    • User-Agent: Information about the software making the HTTP request, which is hashed to identify unique users.
  • Error Logging Information:
    • Timestamp of Errors: The time when an error occurs in the application.
    • Error Details: While we do not collect detailed error information such as exception class names and stack traces, we do record the occurrence of errors for diagnostic purposes.

2. How We Use the Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Service Improvement: To enhance the functionality and performance of N2RSS.
  • User Experience: To ensure the service is meeting the needs of our users by identifying and fixing issues.
  • Usage Statistics: To gather aggregate data on the usage patterns of the service.

3. Data Protection and Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

5. Open-Source Nature

As N2RSS is an open-source project, its source code is publicly available on GitHub at https://github.com/nicopico-dev/n2rss. This transparency allows users to understand how their data is being handled and to contribute to improving the privacy and security measures in place.

6. Your Consent

By using N2RSS, you consent to our Privacy Policy.

7. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and you are encouraged to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting the information we collect.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at n2rss-contact@nicopico.fr.

Conclusion

Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the information you share with us. Thank you for using N2RSS.
